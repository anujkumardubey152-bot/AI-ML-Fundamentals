# AI Ethics & Responsibility

Artificial Intelligence should be developed responsibly.

## Algorithmic Bias

AI systems may produce unfair results if trained on biased data.

## Data Privacy

Personal information should be collected and stored securely.

## Transparency

Users should understand how AI systems make decisions.

## Accountability

Developers are responsible for ensuring AI is safe and fair.

## Responsible AI

Responsible AI focuses on fairness, privacy, security, and transparency.

## Conclusion

Ethics should always be considered while designing AI systems to build trust and protect society.
